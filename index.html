Dourha Maaya

Dourha Maaya is a social & culturel experience held as a project within "Ecole polytechnique Sousse", The aim of this project was to highligh the quality of nature we'have in Tunisia, the importance of it nationaly and for for the sake of global climate and what it would offer in term of health and well-being. In a few words, Dourha Maaya was a challenge started three young students, which goal was simply to survive seven days into the wild with a budget of 100 Dt, relying only on the human instinct of surviving and interacting with local civilians of each town we visit.
Dourha Maaya short movie won two awards later on and was voted one of the most innovative social projects to be held within "Ecoly polytechnique Sousse"
